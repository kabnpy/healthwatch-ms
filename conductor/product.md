# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
End-users at an insurance agency, it allows them to manage client the policies they've taken and handle invoicing and receipts.

## Target Audience
- **Primary Users:** Insurance agency staff and agents.
- **Goal:** To provide a comprehensive management tool for client policies, invoicing, and financial documentation.

## Core Goals
- **Policy Lifecycle Management:** Streamline the management of client policy records, including detailed histories and tracking of renewal dates.
- **Financial Automation:** Automate the generation, delivery, and tracking of client invoices and payment receipts.

## Key Features
- **Client & Policy Database:** A searchable database for client information with full policy history and the ability to attach relevant documents.
- **Automated Alerts:** Email notification system for policy expirations and outstanding invoice reminders.
- **Financial Dashboard:** Real-time visibility into monthly revenue, outstanding balances, and a centralized log for all receipts.
- **Internal Document Generation:** Tools for generating professional internal documents, including invoices and risk notes.

## Visual Identity & UX
- **Modern & Streamlined:** A high-contrast, clean design featuring large interactive elements and simplified navigation to ensure intuitive workflows for document generation and client management.
